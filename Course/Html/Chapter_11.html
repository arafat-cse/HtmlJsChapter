11.3.1 Absolute Lengths

            These are the most popular absolute lengths:
            ● cm : centimeters
            ● mm : millimeters
            ● in : inches (1in = 96px = 2.54cm)
            ● px : pixels (1px = 1/96th of 1in)
            ● pt : points (1pt = 1/72 of 1in)

11.3.2 Relative Lengths
